We provide comprehensive details on the top universities in Pune, helping students make informed decisions about their higher education. Our platform offers insights into university rankings, cutoffs, student reviews, and more.

Universities in Pune

College of Engineering Pune

Location: Shivajinagar, Pune

NIRF Rank: 52

Cutoff: 85%

Reviews: 4.6/5

Founded in 1854, COEP is one of India's oldest engineering colleges. It is renowned for its strong academic programs and vibrant student community, consistently ranking among the top engineering institutions in the country.

Pune Institute of Computer Tech.

Location: Dhankawadi, Pune

NIRF Rank: 201-250

Cutoff: 80%

Reviews: 4.4/5

Established in 1983, PICT is a top private engineering college in Pune, known for its strong focus on computer science and IT education. The college boasts excellent placement records and a competitive academic environment.

Vishwakarma Institute of Tech.

Location: Bibwewadi, Pune

NIRF Rank: 101-150

Cutoff: 75%

Reviews: 4.3/5

VIT Pune, established in 1983, is a well-regarded engineering college offering a wide range of programs. The institute is known for its innovative teaching methods, strong industry connections, and active student life.

MIT World Peace University

Location: Kothrud, Pune

NIRF Rank: 101-150

Cutoff: 75%

Reviews: 4.2/5

MIT World Peace University, established in 1983, is a private university in Pune, India. Renowned for its engineering and management programs, the university emphasizes world peace through education.

Pimpri Chinchwad Clg of Engg.

Location: Pimpri, Pune

NIRF Rank: 251-300

Cutoff: 70%

Reviews: 4.2/5

PCCOE, established in 1999, is a leading engineering college in the Pune region. The college is recognized for its strong emphasis on research and development, student-centric learning, and robust placement opportunities.

Dr. D.Y. Patil Institute of Tech.

Location: Pimpri, Pune

NIRF Rank: 251-300

Cutoff: 65%

Reviews: 4.1/5

Established in 1998, Dr. D.Y. Patil Institute of Technology is a well-known engineering college in Pune. The institute is part of the D.Y. Patil Group, offering quality education with a focus on innovation and practical skills.
